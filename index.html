 Welcome to Australian Fruit Company

 Australian growers have a long standing reputation for producing some of the best fresh fruits, nuts and vegetables in the world. Australia is also  known for its high standards in food safety and quality assurance systems. Australia Fresh is an export initiative that works closely with the  Australian horticulture industry to develop export opportunities so that consumers around the world can enjoy the best that Australia has to offer in  this sector. Strong links with the Australian horticulture industry enables Australia Fresh to place international buyers in contact with leading  Australian growers, industry bodies and reliable exporters. Australia Fresh continues to keep the buyers up to date with information on what's in  season, how the season is shaping up and industry news.

 We are an innovative 100% Australian owned processing enterprise that manufactures and markets high quality fruit based products from a  premium growing region. Our product range includes Jams, Marmalades, Bakers Fillings, Fruit Sauces, Fruit Spreads, Frozen Fruits, Mustards,  Olive Oils, Chutneys, Fruit Pastes, Fruit Condiments and Fruit Juices. AUSSIE GROWERS FRUITS AUSTRALASIA's main business streams are:

  • Manufacturing
  • Contract Manufacturing and/or Packing
  • Foodservice
  • Industrial Supply
