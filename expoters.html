  What we Exports

 Products: Avocados
 Australian Fruit Company (AFC) is one of southern Australia's largest and most experienced avocado packers. AFC packs Hass avocados from  October to December and Fuerte (August / September) and Wurtz (November / December). AFC also packs other avocado varieties; Bacon,  Zutano, Rincon, Reed and Gwen. AFC applies strict post harvest temperature management to ensure all packed avocados are delivered to the customer.
